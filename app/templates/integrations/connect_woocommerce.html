How to get your API credentials

  1. Log in to your WordPress admin dashboard
  2. Go to WooCommerce > Settings > Advanced > REST API
  3. Click "Add key" and set permissions to "Read"
  4. Copy the Consumer Key and Consumer Secret

What happens next?

  1. We'll verify your API credentials
  2. After verification, we'll import your purchase history
  3. Your purchases will appear in your dashboard
  4. You can choose which purchases to share with friends
